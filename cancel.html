Order Cancellation Window

  • Orders may be cancelled within 12 hours of placing the order or before dispatch, whichever is earlier.

How to Cancel

To cancel an order, customers must contact our support team immediately with their Order ID.

Order Cancellation by GODAMN

GODAMN reserves the right to cancel any order due to:

  • Stock unavailability
  • Payment issues
  • Suspected fraudulent activity
  • Incorrect or incomplete customer details

In such cases, a full refund will be initiated to the original payment method.
